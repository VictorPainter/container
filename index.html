About us

Comtainer is a technology company providing all-in-one, on-line, unified platform for the shipment-logistic industry worldwide.
For the first time the entire shipment cycle is live and online.

Importers, Exporters and forwarders can easily compare and find the best offer for shipment and within seconds order and make the payment. Than be updated about every stage of their shipments.

Consolidators on the other end can reach new customers, digitally design the shipment and build the container to maximize profit. Save time, manpower, mistakes, fill your containers to the max, time and time again and Improve efficiency by over 300%.

We are coming from the industry and we are working for the industry.
